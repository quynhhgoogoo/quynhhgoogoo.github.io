Bennu

Using Python 3.

This game is one in our multiple project Hello Bennu,a playground for children includes videos, games and website to inspire and educate them about the world and the Solar System, in NASA App Challenge Vaasa 2018. Everyone can play this game, but our main target group is children. Task of this game is to help spaceship cross as many planet as possible without crashing into obstacles (rocks in this case). While doing tasks, player can also immerse in the beauty of Galaxy through changing pictures taken from NASA Hubble Space Telescope in each destination.

First destination of this game is Earth, then is Mars and final destination is Sun. It teaches children the sequence of planets inside Solar System (e.g Mars is next to Earth). Besides, they will also have a good time while learning new things. Hello Bennu also won the regional NASA challenge and nominated for the Global NASA Space App 2018.

  • Date: October 2018
  • Author : Quynh Luong, Hoang Nguyen, Arttu Nevala, Riku Nevala
  • Category: Game
